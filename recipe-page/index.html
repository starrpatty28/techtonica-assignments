Alicia's Chicken Tortilla Soup

I beleive I make one of the best Tortilla Soup recipes. I learned by looking up a recipe and adding my own flare according to my families taste buds. It is a forever hit in my home and will be passed down to generations.



Chelsea's Messy Apron
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 jalapenos, finely diced
  • 6 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • One 14.5-ounce can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • One 14.5-ounce can black beans, rinced and drained
  • 3 chicken breast, boneless and skinless
  • 2 lines, juiced, plus wedges for garnish
  • 3 tablespoons of cumin
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder (add more if you like spicy
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup of roughly chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 1 bag of tortilla chips (of your choice)
  • 1 avacado, pitted, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded Monterrey cheese

Directions to Tortilla Soup

  1. In a large saucepan heat the vegetable oil
  2. Add the onions and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Once the onions have softened add the garlic and jalepenos and cook for another minute
  4. Pour the chicken broth, tomatoes and beans into the pot and bring to a boil.
  5. Once at a boil lower heat to simmer and add your chicken breasts
  6. Cook the chicken for 20 to 25 minutes
  7. Once chicken is cooked remove from pot
  8. When cool enough to handle shred it and set it aside
  9. Add lime juice and fresh cilantro to the pot

Serving the Tortilla Soup

  1. In a serving bowl add a mound of shredded chicken
  2. Ladle soup over chicken and top with a lime wedge, grilled tortilla strips, avocado slices and cheese
